Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish known for its deliciously crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings. The dough is typically made with flour, water, yeast, and salt, while the sauce is made from fresh tomatoes, garlic, and herbs. A variety of toppings, from pepperoni to vegetables, are added to create a unique flavor experience.

The best pizzas are all about balance—crispy, chewy crust, a flavorful sauce, and the perfect amount of cheese. Whether you prefer a simple Margherita or a fully loaded pie, pizza is a versatile dish that is loved by many worldwide. It's perfect for any occasion, from a casual family dinner to a special celebration.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled dough to a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
  4. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, followed by the Parmesan cheese.
  6. If desired, add pepperoni or any other toppings of your choice.
  7. Carefully slide the parchment paper and pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired.
  10. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes, then slice, garnish with fresh basil, and serve!
